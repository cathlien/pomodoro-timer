What is a pomodoro timer?

The pomodoro technique is a time management method to break work down into small time intervals. After each interval, you take a 3 to 5 minute break. After doing 4 intervals, you take a 15 to 30 minute break, then you repeat from the start! Time intervals are usually 25 minutes, however, you can use this website to customize your own pomodoro timer and set your own breaks.
